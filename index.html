DeepSkr

Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) is one of the mostly common used model for language generation. In this paper, we have implemented and trained three RNN models on the lyrics data set, each with LSTM, stack LSTMs and bidirectional LSTM cell respectively. We choose Maximum Likehood Estimation (MLE) and Schedule Sampling (SS) as the training strategy and use Negative Log Likelihood (NLL) and Bilingual Evaluation Understudy (BLEU) as our evaluation methods. We have also trained model end-to-end so that it does not need any pre-knowledge of word vectors. The goal of this research project is to 1. generate synthesized data (which are lyrics, in our case) 2. compare different structures and training procedure's effect on the quality of outcome.
